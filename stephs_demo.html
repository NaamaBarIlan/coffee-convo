Penguins in the Antarctic Peninsula are feeling the effects of climate change as their world warms. When it warms, it rains. When it rains, penguin chicks die. You can help by insulating your house, reuse items when possible, carpoool, etc. Every time you reuse and item a penguin chick lives!
